    Coorg is a heavenly place of peace and scenic beauty, surrounded by coffee estates, lush green paddy fields and water sources. People of Coorg have thier own unique culuture and they are well know for bravery and hospitality. Would you like to explore in this paradise of nature ? step down to Gonicoppal- a small town in the center of Coorg, from where you can reach whole the coorg in few stretches. The sweet home "Baby Cottage" lies in the heart of Gonicoppal town.

    Baby Cottage or Baby Homestay is one among several homestays of Coorg which is situated in Gonicoppal. Once your settled here, its an hour drive to Irpu falls,Nagarahole, Mrathunjaya temple etc. Since Gonicoppal comes exactly in the center of Coorg, its convenient to reach all the places of Coorg. Baby Cottage is a home of your own where you can cook and dine. Its a well furnished house of two bedrooms, a Drawing room, a dinning hall and an independent kitchen, where you can cook your own meal or even get into restaurants near by.
